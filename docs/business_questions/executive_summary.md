# Executive Summary

## Project Overview

This project was developed to build an end-to-end analytics engineering and business intelligence solution for historical NIFTY 50 stock market analysis using SQL Server and Power BI.

The solution consolidates fragmented multi-company stock datasets into a centralized analytical platform capable of:
- volatility analysis,
- anomaly detection,
- KPI reporting,
- and interactive market intelligence visualization.

---

# Business Objective

The primary objective was to simulate a real-world analytics engineering workflow capable of:
- processing large-scale historical stock data,
- identifying abnormal market behavior,
- analyzing company-level volatility trends,
- and delivering actionable business insights through interactive dashboards.

---

# Technical Solution

The project implemented:
- automated SQL-based ETL pipelines,
- staging and dimensional modeling architecture,
- analytical SQL transformations,
- rolling volatility calculations,
- anomaly detection logic,
- and Power BI semantic reporting layers.

The system successfully processed:
- 233,000+ historical stock market records,
- across 45 NIFTY 50 companies,
- covering trading periods from 2000–2023.

---

# Key Analytical Capabilities

The dashboard solution provides:
- rolling 30-day volatility analysis,
- Top-N volatile company rankings,
- anomaly detection insights,
- market movement tracking,
- interactive filtering,
- and executive KPI reporting.

Dynamic Power BI interactions allow users to explore:
- company-level behavior,
- historical volatility trends,
- and abnormal market movement periods.

---

# Business Value

The analytical platform enables:
- improved market risk visibility,
- comparative company analysis,
- volatility monitoring,
- and interactive financial analytics exploration.

The project demonstrates how analytics engineering and business intelligence workflows can transform raw market datasets into actionable analytical insights.

---

# Final Outcome

The final solution successfully combined:
- SQL Server data engineering,
- analytical SQL processing,
- dimensional modeling,
- and Power BI dashboard development

into a portfolio-ready end-to-end analytics project.
